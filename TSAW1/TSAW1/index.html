Public Safety

An eye in the sky makes all the difference

Benefits of having Eagle Eye

Make Better Decision

Warrants, raids, barricades, and hostage situations are some of the most dangerous operations for police officers. An aerial perspective can help officers gain critical situational awareness while maintaining a safe standoff distance.

TSAW is ideal for providing critical monitoring of any high-risk situation where decisions need to be made quickly and can remain on-station to stream live multicast video to officers and command posts simultaneously.

Find People Faster

With TSAW operations, the sooner search efforts start, the better the chance of a successful outcome. TSAW Drones can be airborne within minutes of first responders arriving on scene. Pre-planned flight paths and Vector motion tracking enable the operator to easily fly a grid search in a given area and automatically spot moving objects. Both infrared and daylight cameras help spot people hidden in tree cover and rough terrain.

Direct Aid where it's needed most

From forest fires, to hurricanes, to tornadoes, first responders need to be prepared to quickly assemble a response in the aftermath of a natural disaster. TSAW Drones enables rescue teams to fly before other manned aircraft arrive on scene, quickly assessing damage, locating survivors and providing the intelligence needed to determine where aid is needed most.

Find People Faster

TSAW Drones enables the persistent observation and monitoring of a fire and can fly when darkness or smoke prevents manned aircraft or ground crews from getting near a blaze. The EO/IR MK-II camera is a vital tool to help you see through smoke and monitor the fire’s spread, so you can quickly visualize your plan of attack, locate hot spots, and save lives.

Direct Aid where it's needed most

Investigating a collision requires a fine balance between two competing demands – the requirement for collecting thorough, lasting evidence and the need to open roads as quickly as possible. The R60 provides a full range of aerial mapping and automated point cloud solutions that enable first responders and investigation teams to map and clear a site up to 4 times faster than with a Total Station or traditional measures.

Real Life Application of First Responder Drones

Drones are quickly becoming a very commonly used tool by many public safety agencies. Police, Fire, and SAR teams benefit from the rapid deployment of drones. Within minutes of arrival, a drone can stream real-time aerial footage to a remote location for areas or conditions where it is difficult or impossible to fly manned aircrafts. With the addition of thermal cameras, drones can provide visibility through smoke or dense foliage to quickly identify hot spots unseen by regular cameras.
